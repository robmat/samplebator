“Creating enterprise optimisation through versatile, innovative project control solutions”

As part of its continuous drive for innovation on behalf of its Client base and commitment to developing a world class project controls environment, Rhead Group have now developed an Intelligent 24/7 performance measurement mobile application (App) for clients to further support their Project Control operations.

The App remotely connects to project data over live mobile networks, anywhere in the world delivering significant business intelligence to project managers thereby enabling monitoring and trouble-shooting on the go.
