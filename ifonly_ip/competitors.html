Behind the "If Only" app there is an opportunity for all designers to develop product design solutions for everyday problems faced by people with long term chronic conditions. The competition will offer a £5,000 prize for the most innovative product designed - this is an opportunity to test your design skills and make a difference. If you're up for the challenge read on.

The aim is to get people with chronic conditions to upload video demonstrations sharing their experiences of the practical problems with everyday objects. These videos are viewable on our website and through this app. Designers must first view these videos, and then develop an original product that could solve any of the issues demonstrated.

We encourage designers to draw upon their creative talents using novel ideas, materials and techniques to design remarkable products. Designers are asked to send their blue prints, plans and descriptions via the if Only website - if Only website - www.ifonlyitworked.com

The winning product will be decided by a panel of experts and the competition will provide a £5,000 prize for the most innovative product designed.
